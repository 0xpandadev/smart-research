# Strategy And Investment Lenses

Use these lenses for market, competitive, diligence, and board-level research.

## Market Structure

Assess:

- Market boundaries and segments
- Growth drivers
- Profit pools
- Value chain and control points
- Switching costs
- Supply/demand constraints
- Regulation and standards
- Regional differences

## Competitive Advantage

Look for:

- Distribution advantage
- Data advantage
- Brand/trust
- Network effects
- Ecosystem control
- Cost advantage
- Regulatory moat
- Integration/workflow lock-in
- Execution capacity

## Winner/Loser Logic

Explain:

- Who gains control?
- Who loses margin?
- Who becomes commoditized?
- What bottleneck moves?
- What second-order effect matters?
- What leading indicators would confirm the thesis?

## Investment Quality

Separate:

- Attractive market
- Attractive company
- Attractive price
- Attractive timing

Do not infer investment attractiveness from market growth alone.
