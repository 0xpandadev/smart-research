# Web Marketing Lenses

Use when the research involves SEO, paid ads, landing pages, conversion funnels, social content, creator marketing, brand positioning, audience demand, or digital growth.

## Demand And Intent

Inspect:

- search intent and query language
- category keywords, long-tail pain language, and comparison queries
- seasonality and trend changes
- audience segments and jobs-to-be-done
- questions people ask before buying

## Funnel And Creative Review

Look for:

- traffic sources
- landing page promise
- offer and pricing clarity
- proof, testimonials, case studies, trust markers
- objections handled or ignored
- call-to-action friction
- competitor message positioning

## Social And Community Signals

Extract:

- repeated hooks and angles that get engagement
- pain language from comments, reviews, Reddit, X, HN, forums, and YouTube
- creator/influencer narratives
- objections, skepticism, and backlash
- memes, shorthand, and phrases users naturally repeat

## Output Checks

- Separate audience language from factual claims.
- Do not treat virality as proof of durable demand.
- Identify which signal affects awareness, consideration, conversion, retention, or advocacy.
- Suggest content angles only when requested; otherwise preserve evidence and patterns.
