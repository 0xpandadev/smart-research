# Research Ledger

Use a research ledger for long, deep, or wild investigations.

## Ledger Sections

- Research question
- Working assumptions
- Issue tree
- Hypotheses
- Source map
- Evidence table
- Original excerpts and table values
- Contradictions
- Weak signals
- Open questions
- Next searches

## Evidence Table

Use:

`Claim | Type | Source | Date | Evidence | Confidence | Contradictions | Next Check`

Types:

- `Fact`
- `Estimate`
- `Inference`
- `Signal`
- `Risk`
- `Open question`

## Context Hygiene

For long work, keep the ledger concise. Do not paste whole articles or large copied source text. Preserve source links, page numbers, table identifiers, and short evidence notes.
