# Business And Product Lenses

Use these lenses to make research useful beyond summary.

## Competitive Reality

Consider:

- Direct competitors
- Indirect competitors
- Status quo
- Manual workaround
- Excel/spreadsheets
- Internal build
- Services/consultants
- Open source alternatives
- "Do nothing"

## Product-Market Fit

Look for:

- Pull from customers, not just vendor push
- Retention or repeated use
- Budget owner clarity
- Urgent pain versus nice-to-have
- Migration from workaround to product
- Community enthusiasm and complaints
- Case studies with measurable outcomes

## Pricing And Monetization

Inspect:

- Pricing metric
- Packaging
- Buyer and budget
- Expansion path
- Gross margin implications
- Sales cycle and procurement friction
- Competitive price anchors

## Startup/Opportunity Lens

Ask:

- Why now?
- What changed in technology, regulation, distribution, cost, or behavior?
- What is the wedge?
- Who is the first desperate user?
- What incumbent constraint creates room?
- What distribution path is plausible?

## AI Product Lens

For AI-related topics:

- Is AI core to value or just interface?
- What workflow does it replace or augment?
- What data advantage exists?
- How are quality, latency, cost, privacy, and evaluation handled?
- What breaks in production?
